Invisible Women

Author

Caroline Criado Perez is a British writer and activist who has spent much of her career fighting for gender equality and representation. She’s well-known for leading campaigns like getting Jane Austen and for raising awareness about how women are often overlooked in public life.

The Book

Her book Invisible Women: Exposing Data Bias in a World Designed for Men (2019) dives deep into the hidden ways our world is built around a “default male,” leaving women at a disadvantage. She highlights how everything from healthcare and workplace safety to transportation and technology often fails to consider women’s needs simply because data about women is missing or ignored

Impact

The impact of this book has been huge—it not only became a bestseller but also started important conversations about gender bias in design, research, and policy-making. For many readers, it was eye-opening, making them see everyday life differently and understand how deeply these biases affect women’s lives
